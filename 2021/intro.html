WebAIM screen reader user survey: structure

When trying to find information on a lengthy web page, which of the following are you most likely to do first?
Response% of respondents
Navigate through the headings on the page67.7%
Use the Find feature13.9%
Navigate through the links of the page7.1%
Read through the page8.1%
Navigate through the landmarks/regions of the page3.2%

Headings?

For structure, not visual weight!
  • H1: Headings Create the Page Outline
    • H2: They outline major topics
      • H3: And subtopics
      • H3: And related subtopics
        • H4: And something nested with more depth
        • H4: And something else
    • H2: And then introduce another major topic
      • H3: It might have subtopics too

Screen reader users rely on heading levels

When navigating a web page by headings, how useful are the heading levels (e.g., "Heading 1", "Heading 2", etc.) to you?
Response% of respondents
Very useful52.8%
Somewhat useful32.9%
Not very useful9.6%
Not at all useful1.8%
I don't know2.9%
