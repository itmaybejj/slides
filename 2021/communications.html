For reference: selected WCAG requirements for legibility

"All non-text content that is presented to the user has a text alternative that serves the equivalent purpose..."
"Images of text are only used for pure decoration or where a particular presentation of text is essential to the information being conveyed."
"...Except for captions and images of text, text can be resized without assistive technology up to 200 percent without loss of content or functionality."
"Content can be presented without loss of information or functionality, and without requiring scrolling in two dimensions for... Vertical scrolling content at a width equivalent to 320 CSS pixels...
"The visual presentation of text and images of text has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1, except...Large-scale text...[may] have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1..."
"Instructions provided for understanding and operating content do not rely solely on sensory characteristics of components such as shape, color, size, visual location, orientation, or sound."
