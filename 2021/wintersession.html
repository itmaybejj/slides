Structural elements really matter for assistive tech

Experience 1

Screen reader users like to:
* Navigate by headings.
* Skim using links and page structure.
* Don't like having to lean back and listen to the whole page.

Experience 2

Screen readers users like to:

  • Navigate by headings.
  • Skim using links and page structure.
  • Not have to lean back and listen to a wall of text.
